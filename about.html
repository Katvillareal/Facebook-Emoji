6 Simple Steps
Step 1. Mold the clay into any design your desire
Step 2. Insert an eye pin needle clay model
Step 3. Bake in the oven (Refer to clay packet for heat temperature and time)
Step 4. Remove from oven and wait until the clay has cooled down
Step 5. Attach chain to the eye pin needle
Step 6. Attach chain to the key ring
